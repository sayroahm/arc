Before You Begin
Code of Conduct

By proceeding, you acknowledge that all content is strictly confidential and protected under the Copyright Act of 1976 (Title 17, U.S. Code). Unauthorized reproduction, capture, or distribution of any testing material is strictly prohibited and may result in immediate removal, test invalidation, and civil penalties ranging from $750 to $30,000 per infringed work.

Test Instructions

Read each question carefully before selecting your answer. You may navigate freely between questions using the arrow buttons in the toolbar. All questions must be answered before submitting. You will be shown any unanswered or flagged questions before final submission.

Using the Toolbar

← / → — Navigate between questions.
🖊 Highlight — Click to activate, then select text to highlight it yellow.
■ Mask — Opens a draggable black panel to cover content you have already read.
🗒 Notepad — Opens a scratch notepad for working through problems. Notes are not saved.
⚑ Flag — Marks the current question for review. Flagged questions appear in the end-test summary.
? Help — Opens this reference at any time.

Unauthorized Exit

Exiting full screen without authorization will trigger a 20-minute access suspension. Personnel will be notified. Do not attempt to open other applications, developer tools, or browser menus during the test.
